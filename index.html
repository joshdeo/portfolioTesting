THE COME UP
I was born in Staten Island, New York in 1996. I shortly moved cities to Queens where I was raised for the majority of my youth which set the foundation to how I think, speak and observe many aspects in my life. I learned not only street smarts, but also about food, fashion, and multiple other things

In 2005 my mom and I moved to Fort Lauderdale, Florida for an escape and change of scenery. Miami had everything New York had, plus warm weather. It was like heaven on earth. My mom and I would walk down Miami Beach everyday after school. I vividly remember walking by Versace's Mansion and seeing a myriad of supercars. I was there through Hurricane Katrina, that was a sight I can’t unsee.

I now reside in Toronto. I’ve lived here the past 10 years and grew into the adult I am today. Throughout my journey I learned a lot about not only myself but about the quality of people that I embarked. Here is where my love for videography and graphic design came about. I began by filming and editing Call of Duty videos to stream online. I later studied Journalism and soon began my internship at Discovery Channel, that led to my career there for the next two years.
