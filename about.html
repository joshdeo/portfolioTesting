About Me

Discovery Channel Canada

All the content you see on my website is from my time at Discovery channel Canada's flagship Science and Technology show 'Daily Planet'. I had an internship there as part of a college culminating assignment and was fortunate enough to be offered a job a few months later. The show first aired in 1995 and I started working there straight out of college, from October 2016 until the show ended in May 2018. I began as a researcher and slowly worked my way up to producing mini pieces and then chase producing which was an absolute thrill.

By the time I was chase producing I had learned a lot about journalism. The job was nonstop, which I loved. I would get into the office by 8 a.m and sometimes work a full 12 hour day - but it was worth every minute. It went from office work to waking up at 3 a.m for an interview with a crew in Finland. I couldn't even begin to explain what a language barrier at three in the morning sounds like. But I still managed to get into the office and edit it later that morning.

Apple

In August 2018 I started my job at Apple. It's an incredible place to work; you meet great people, both staff and customers. You not only learn about yourself, you acquire important people skills. The retail space is such a fast paced environment and easy to get lost in the moment with adrenaline. It definitely teaches multi-tasking and time management skills. I started in the Product area in sales, then moved up to the Genius Bar. Daily Planet was a Science and Technology show, so being around technology all day is my forte. Solving the issues that customer's have is a great ordeal, but an even better feeling when you know they walked away satisfied. I now am a repair technician based in-store, which is a great job to have and I do not take it lightly.

Hobbies

A massive part of my life is Technology. My love for it stems from growing up with most the GameBoy's and consoles. After that it was all about having the latest iPhone. I am currently a self-taught web developer. I enjoy learning new technologies everyday and more about computers, phones and how others are implementing it to help better the world. These technologies include the same primary three I used to develop this website - HTML, CSS and JAVASCRIPT.
